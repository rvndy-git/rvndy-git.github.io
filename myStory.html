My Story

Hi, my name is Randy, I’m a starting junior at BECA. I’ve taken classes in basic HTML in 7th grade and Python in my sophomore year in high school. Along with Watching videos as well as referencing some websites for self-tutoring, I’ve grown to acknowledge myself as a learner and I take pride in my growth. I’m fascinated by the idea of creating things in the line tech especially videogames. I visualize a lot and I want those things to come to fruition

Along with playing videogames, i enjoy playing basketball and i've become very attached to the sneaker culture because of it. My favorite pair of sneakers might be the bred 4's and my second pick would be the 1's. I don't own many shoes and i don't consider myself a sneakerhead. I wear all my shoes. i only resell my shoes when they're really expensive on secondary markets. Then those sneakers would be deadstock and never worn even if i really wanted it. Although Michael jordan might not be my favorite player, his sneaker line with with nike was really popular. It's even made its way from the being called basketball shoes to being noticed in the fasion world. Making big collaborations with OFF-White, Levi's Kaws and more.
